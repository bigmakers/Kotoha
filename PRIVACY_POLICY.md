# Privacy Policy - Kotoha (言波)

Last updated: February 23, 2026

## Overview

Kotoha (言波) is a real-time subtitle application that converts speech to text. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**Kotoha does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### Speech Data
- Speech recognition is processed using Apple's Speech framework on your device.
- Audio data is sent to Apple's servers for speech recognition processing as part of Apple's standard Speech framework operation.
- We do not access, store, or transmit any speech data ourselves.

### No Analytics or Tracking
- We do not use any analytics tools.
- We do not track user behavior.
- We do not collect usage statistics.

### No Third-Party Services
- We do not integrate any third-party SDKs or services that collect data.

## Permissions

### Microphone Access
- The app requires microphone access solely for real-time speech recognition.
- Microphone audio is processed in real-time and is not recorded or stored by the app.

### Speech Recognition
- The app uses Apple's built-in Speech Recognition framework.
- Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for information about how Apple processes speech data.

## Data Storage
- All app settings (theme, color preferences) are stored locally on your device.
- No data is stored on external servers.

## Children's Privacy
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
- We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
